Homemate - Effective hourly service solution

A flexible and efficient service model, specifically designed to meet the diverse needs of users in today's society. Instead of committing to long-term contracts or paying a large sum for hiring household help on a fixed schedule, this model offers excellent flexibility. Users can arrange the hourly service schedule to suit their own time and needs. This brings significant benefits to individuals with busy schedules or those who need the flexibility of home cleaning. You can choose a specific time for the house help to come and work, and only pay for the actual time the service is used. This helps save costs and optimize efficiency, providing convenience and time savings for the users.

The reasons for choosing Homemate

Professionalism

The employees sent to you have been carefully screened and trained by us before being officially employed to serve.

Security and Privacy

All of your personal information is kept secure and guaranteed. We encrypt all user data. We also provide information about vaccine status, health, as well as background information about the service personnel before they arrive at your home.

Efficiently

Within 2 to 12 hours from the start of the service, we will call to confirm and send the service personnel to clean.

Connection

The selected service will be sent directly to you for confirmation in advance to avoid any misunderstandings and discrepancies. Additionally, throughout the service usage, you will be continuously updated with information regarding the order status.

Cost Savings

Compared to hiring a house help on a fixed schedule or a long-term contract, Homemate allows you to only pay for the actual time the service is used. This helps you save a significant amount of money.

Suited for the Modern Society

In today's society, people often have busy schedules and limited time to manage household chores. Homemate understands and addresses this need by providing flexible services that align with your schedule and individual requirements, helping to solve this common issue.
